Samantha Gornstein

Send Me A Message

Send Me A Message

    For over two decades, Samantha Gornstein has been a business owner in West Palm Beach and Boynton Beach. Samantha comes to Douglas Elliman with an extensive background in public relations and marketing. She brings an entrepreneurial spirit, exceptional client service, and an unyielding commitment to excellence into the luxury real estate market.

    Raised in New York and Boca Raton, Samantha’s early career commenced in New York City, where she concentrated on strategic communication and brand positioning, laying the foundation for success in the highly competitive world of luxury brands.

    As a member of The Edmund Bogen Team, “I am committed to connecting buyers with their dream homes and maximizing seller returns by achieving the best possible outcomes for their estates.”

    Samantha’s career is a testament to the power of blending passion with knowledge. She currently resides in Lake Worth with her husband and two sons.
